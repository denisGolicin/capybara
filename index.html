ISLAMIC COIN

Task

Create an incentive for the most loyal community members and stimulate the Pontem products' usage.

Result

We used alI to generate references for the Pontem space pirate and minted 1000 unique NFTs with different rareness. We had 140k form submissions, then compared wallets to those that engaged with liquidswap and qualified to participate in the giveaway. Also, we have excluded repeating wallets. After that, we had 30k wallets selected. From there, we have chosen 1000 random wallets (Alejandro used an excel formula for random selection) and have given them a chance to mint NFT in 1 hour. Then we selected another 1000 randomly, and so on. It was free minting with 0 royalties, all for the community. Within the first 2 hours, all collection was claimed, and the second market trading volume exceeded 140k $, making it the top collection on the marketplace. Space Pirates remain in the top 10 collections list with the highest floor price.
